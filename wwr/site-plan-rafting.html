Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote a white water rafting business. It will tell people what services the company offers along with their prices, when the company runs trips, and other pertinent information.

Audience

The target audience is complete beginners through intermediate riders. This will be anyone who needs guidance and teaching in a white-water rafting experience. The site will mostly cater to large groups who already know each other. This includes work groups, school groups, and church groups.

Style Guide

Color Palette

The primary-color is Marian blue, #1c448e. This will represent the deep blue of water.

The secondary-color is Light cyan, #C6F4Fa. This represents the lighter blue of wave tops.

The accent1-color is Hunter green #2d6233 which represents the green of the forest the river runs through.

The accent2-color is Turquoise, #00ccb1, which represents the green blue of river water.
